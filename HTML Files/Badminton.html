Badminton

Introduction


Badminton is a sport which is playing with a raquet. Players should hit to the Shuttlecock with the racket and the shuttlecock should go across the net. As the minimus players are only two, And the Maximum players who can play this sport is four. Which we called Singles(One player - One side) and Doubles (Two players - One side). We play badminton oftenly inside area. Court shape is just like a rectangle. To get the points we should land the shuttlecock inside of opponents area. If it goes out Umpire will give one mark to other team. This is the basic idea of Badminton Sport.


This sport was developed by British India. Since 1992 Badminton has been playing as a sport in Summer Olympic games. So the basic event types of this sport is Mens Singles, Mens Doubles, Womens Singles, Womens Dooubles and Mixed Doubles. Mixed Doubles was added after four years. Players should improve their Strenth, Speed and also the Techniqes to Win those Tournments.

Rules

Basically when two players are playing (One player each side), Service should go cross. Which means if you're in Right side the service of the shuttle should land at the Left side. When you're the one who doing the service, If your mark is a even number you should start service from Right side. And also if your mark is Odd number, You should start service from Left side. There a Rule Called Short Service. If a Player put the serve short (below 1.98m) It'll be a Short Service. So one Point will go to Opponent side. Point Struture is only one point. You can't get two points at once. And when you're playing singles your court is only dealing with 152cm's. Which means Side 46cm lines are Out lines. When playing with Doubles you can Deal with the whole court. When you're playing a Doubles Tournment Your all services cannot put more than 6.10m. The it'll be a Long Service.
